Dubai is United Arab Emirates

Dubai is a city and emirate located in the Persian Gulf. With a population of around 3 million, Dubai is one of the seven emirates of the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Dubai has become one of the fastest-growing and wealthiest cities in the world in recent years. In particular, Dubai is known for its unique architecture, such as the Burj Khalifa - the tallest building in the world, Burj Al Arab - the world's first "7-star" hotel, the world's largest shopping center - Dubai Mall, and the world-class entertainment park - Dubai Parks and Resorts. Dubai is also known for its prime geographical location between Asia, Europe, and Africa, attracting millions of tourists from all over the world. Dubai is also an international hub for finance and business, with numerous investment and business opportunities for companies.
